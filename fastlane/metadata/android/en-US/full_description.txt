<p>Tack is a modern metronome app for Android with a beautifully designed interface that has all the features you need for practicing a music piece accurate to the beat.</p>

<p><b>Features:</b></p><ul>
   <li>Beautiful beat visualization with subdivisions and changeable emphasizes</li>
   <li>BPM bookmarks as app shortcuts</li>
   <li>Options for count in, incremental tempo change, song duration and swing</li>
   <li>Settings for flash screen, volume boost, audio latency correction and elapsed time</li>
   <li>Dynamic color and dynamic contrast support</li>
   <li>Large screen support</li>
   <li>No ads or analytics</li>
</ul>
